VACANZA'20

While we all try to make the best out of our vacation, we often find ourselves lost and bored in these extended leisure hours we have. Why not to utilise this time and make it fruitful just by sitting at home! To make your summers entertaining, Team CSI is coming up with an event bagged with ample excitement, exploration, teasure and enjoyment named, VACANZA'20

ROUND 1


Round 1 comprises of four domains from which participants have to select atleast two domains and can choose maximum of four domains for more points. The more domain you opt for, the more you score.

The four domains are:

Vintage Photography | Leaf Collage | Origami Art | Face Mask Art

ROUND 2


Test your knowledge just by answering some basic questions and gain points easily. Round 2 contains quiz questions based on popular TV series and sports.

The fields are as follows:

Football | Stranger Things | Cricket | F.R.I.E.N.D.S



ROUND 3


The final round consists of making an interesting and astonishing magic trick video which might force people to say "How did you do that!". The best and the most surprising video will be selected as the winner.
